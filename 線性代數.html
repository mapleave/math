向量空間定義

V is a vector space over F
(-1) For all x、y ∈ V , x+y ∈ V (加法有封閉性)
(0) For all x ∈ V c ∈ F , cx ∈ V (純量乘法有封閉性)
(1) For all x、y ∈ V , x+y=y+x (可交換)
(2) For all x、y、z ∈ V , (x+y)+z=x+(y+z) (可結合)
(3) For all x ∈ V , 存在 0 ∈ V s.t. 0+x=x (存在加法單位元素)
(4) For all x ∈ V , 存在 y ∈ V s.t. x+y=0 (存在加法反元素)
(5) For all x ∈ V , 存在 1 ∈ F s.t. 1x=x (存在scalar的單位元素)
(6) For all x ∈ V , a、b ∈ F , a(bx)=(ab)x (結合律)
(7) For all x、y ∈ V , a ∈ F, a(x+y)=ax+ay (分配律)
(8) For all x ∈ V , a、b ∈ F , (a+b)x=ax+bx (分配律)

消去法(Thm1.1)

Let V be a vector space over F
If x,y,z ∈ V(F) s.t. x+y=x+z, then y=z
Pf: There exists a vector v in V s.t. x+v=0
y=y+0=y+(x+v)=(x+y)+v=(x+z)+v=(x+v)+z=0+z=z
(已知:x+y=x+z,v是x的加法反元素,把v當作-x看)
推論一: The zero vector is unique
pf: If 0 and 0' are zero vector in V
then 0=0+0=0+0' => 0=0' by Thm1.1
推論二: The inverse of a vector u is unique
pf: v,v' be inverse of u in V, i.e. u+v=0 u+v'=0
v=v+0=v+(u+v')=(v+u)+v'=0+v'=v'

(Thm1.2)

In any V be a vector space over F,the following statements are true:
(1) 0x=0 ∀x∈V (2) a0=0 ∀a∈F
(3) (-a)u=-(au)=a(-u), ∀a∈F ∀u∈V
pf: (1) 0x=(0+0)x=0x+0x ∴ 0=0x
(2) a0=a(0+0)=a0+a0 ∴ 0=a0
(3) ∵ -(au) is the inverse of au and
au+(-a)u=(a+(-a))u=0u=0
au+a(-u)=a(u+(-u))=a0=0
∴(-a)u, a(-u) are also inverse of au
=> -(au)=(-a)u=a(-u) by uniqueness

子空間定義

Def: Let V be a vector space over F
A subset W of V, is called a subspace of V
If W(F) is also a vector space with the same two operations
Remark: 只需要check 向量空間定義的(-1) (0) (3) (4)
Thm1.3: Let V be a vector space and W a subset of V.
Then W is a subspace of V<=>the following three conditions
hold for the operations defined in V.
(a) 0∈W (b) x+y∈W whenever x∈W and y∈W
(c) cx∈W whenever c∈F and x∈W
In fact, W is nonempty set, then (a) is not even needed.
Pf: (=>)If W is a subspace of V, then W is a vector space with
the operations of addition and multiplication defind on V.
Hence conditions (b) and (c) hold
there exists a vector 0'∈W s.t. 0'+x=x ∀x∈W
But also x+0=x, thus 0'=0 by Thm1.1. So condition(a) holds.
(<=) (只剩需證明(4)) ∀x∈W⊂V => ∃ -x∈V with x+(-x)=0
∵ -x=(-1)x∈W by (c) (∴ W exist 加法反元素)
=> W is a subspace of V

(Thm1.4)

Wα:subspace of V , α∈S, V:a vector space
=>∩Wα is a subspace of V
Pf: x,y∈∩Wα => x,y∈Wα ∀α∈S => x+y∈Wα ∀α∈S
by Thm1.3 (∵Wα is a subspace of V)
=> x+y∈∩Wα

線性組合定義

Def: Let V be a vector space over F and S a nonempty subset of V
A vector v∈V is called a linear combination of vectors in S
v=a1u1+......+anun for some u1...un∈S and a1...an∈F
Def: Let S be a nonempty subset of a vector space V.
The span of S, denoted span(S)
Def: span(∅)={0}
Def: A subset S of a vector space V , if span(S)=V
we also say that the vectors of S span V.

(Thm1.5)

S is the subset of V
(a) span(S) is a subspace of V
(b) span(S) is the smallest subspace containing S
(i.e. if W is a subspace of V and W⊃S, then W⊃span(S) )
Pf of (a): ∃ ai∈F,ui∈S i=1...n, ∃ bi∈F,vi∈S i=1...m
with x=∑aiui, y=∑bivi x,y∈span(S)
x+y=a1u1+...+anun+b1v1+...+bmvm ∈span(S) (by Def)
Likewise, c∈F cx=ca1u1+...+canun ∈span(S) (by Def)
Pf of (b): Wanted: if x∈span(S) then x∈W.
∃ ui∈S, ai∈F i=1...n s.t. x=∑aiui
Since ui∈S =>ui∈W (∵W⊃S)
Since W is a subspace (子空間定義) => x∈W =>Done!
